Permissions
===========

General
-------

Permissions is defined in terms of user, group and roles.

A group may contain subgroups and users. A role is essentially a group, but in the context of a folder node.

Dynamic Permissions
-------------------

A dynamic permission is defined on a node for a specific group where a permission is available when a specific condition is true.
